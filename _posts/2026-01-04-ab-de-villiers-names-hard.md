"De Villiers yaar, bade bhai!

AB de Villiers hame ek aisa call deta hai, jo hamari T20 World Cup defence ko ek hi way se rakh sakti hai. Iske saath, hamari team mein ek bahut hi power player hain, jo hamari chances ko badalti hai.

Hardik Pandya, woh ek match-winner hain, jaanta hai. Yeh tabhi hota hai, jab hamare team ke khiladi ko upar jashn diya jata hai. Iske sath, hamare bowlers bhi bahut hi shaktishali hain, jo hamare team ko scoring game mein madad karte hain.

AB de Villiers ne bahut hi sach mein yeh call deta hai, ki Hardik Pandya woh pivotal player hain, jo hamari team ko win karega. Inke saath, hamari team ko aur bhi strong banane mein madad karega. Kaafi rahna hai, tab tak ki hum har ek ball ko apni upar jashn ki or phir se khel sakte hain."
