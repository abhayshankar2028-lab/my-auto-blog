In a thrilling conclusion to the ILT20 season, the Desert Vipers secured their maiden title by defeating the Mumbai Indians in a nail-biting finish. The decisive match came down to a cliffhanger as MIE pushed their target to 150/2. However, the Vipers' bowling depth proved too much for Mumbai, with their bowlers dominating the line-up.

In a masterclass display, the Vipers' trio of Anuj Malhotra, Tanmay Chandila, and Ansh Gul revealed their exceptional skills, sharing the spoils and earning the Desert Vipers their first-ever title. The Vipers' victory marked a significant milestone in the ILT20 season, capping an incredible campaign that left fans eagerly anticipating next year's tournament. The Desert Vipers, led by an inspired Anuj Malhotra, look set to return stronger than ever.
