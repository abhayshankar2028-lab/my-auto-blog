In a candid interview, Neena Gupta opened up about her most memorable film, Saath Saath (1999). The movie, starring Shah Rukh Khan and Preity Zinta, catapulted Neena Gupta to stardom. However, during the film's production, Neena Gupta expressed her regret over the title 'Saath Saath'. She believes that the title overshadowed her character, making it difficult for her to be seen as a heroine. Neena Gupta attributed her biggest mistake to the title's impact on the film's narrative. The actress further emphasized that the experience taught her a valuable lesson about how to handle her career and not let external factors affect her self-worth. Neena's honest insights may provide insight into a common misconception that many actresses face in the Bollywood industry.
