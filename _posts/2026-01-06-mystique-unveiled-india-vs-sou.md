The Indian squad, led by the ever-reliable Ravindra Jadeja, boasts a formidable lineup that includes the likes of Ishan Kishan and Devendra Wadheria, while the South African team, spearheaded by the dynamic AB de Villiers, is known for its unorthodox approach to the game.

As the two teams take to the field, the atmosphere is electric, with the air thick with anticipation.
