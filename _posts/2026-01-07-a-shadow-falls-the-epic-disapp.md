In an explosion of laughter and tears, The Simpsons' beloved Duffman, the show's unapologetic mouthy mascot, has vanished. The crepuscular glow of his iconic red and yellow suit has faded, leaving an eerie void in the hearts of fans worldwide. Thirty years of irreverent escapades with great Homer, Marge, and Bart have cumulatively crafted an unshakeable legacy. Duffman's interlude in the Springfield universe was always an uneven but endearing dance – but now, the stage stands still. As whispers of "what did he do?" resound across the globe, debate rages on – will Duffman's long-lost secrets unravel, or will the void left be sealed forever?
