In the shadows, a fire burns. The remnants of a family's legacy still smoldering after the devastating loss of their patriarch. Beneath the gleaming surface of their current lives, ripples of discontent simmer, waiting to be unleashed. 

A Yakuza warlord has emerged, determined to claim the very soul of their family's dark past. The web of deceit and corruption is intricate, but one thing remains clear: the only way to restore honor is to confront the enemy head-on.

Jason Momoa's stoic determination and Dave Bautista's unyielding ferocity will be put to the test as they embark on a perilous journey of revenge. The stakes are high, the battle is fierce, and the true meaning of family will be tested like never before.

As the Yakuza's grip tightens, the once-unbreakable bonds between brothers begin to fray. Loyalty is tested, and the line between loyalty and hate blurs. The question remains: will the past be redeemed, or will the present be consumed by the shadows of their family's dark legacy?
