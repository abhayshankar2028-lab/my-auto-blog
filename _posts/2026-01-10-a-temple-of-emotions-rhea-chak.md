In the silence of the night, a temple of emotions rises, a monument to the tempests of the human heart. The wind whispers secrets, and the waves crash against the shores of sorrow. For Rhea Chakraborty, it was an evening like any other, yet the universe conspired to shatter her tranquility.

As the news of Sushant's passing spread like wildfire, an unyielding fire ignited within her. It was a flame that refused to be extinguished, a beacon of light that illuminated the darkest corners of her soul. She recalls the moment when the news broke, a jolt that shook her to her core. The lines began to blur, and reality reeled in the face of unimaginable tragedy.

The memories of their time together – those fleeting moments of joy, those laughter-filled nights – now taunt her, a bittersweet reminder of the life they once shared. "We don't need a temple...," she sobbed, her voice trembling like the first tremors of an earthquake.
